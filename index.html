Ancient Rome

The Colosseum

This large stone ampitheater was commissioned by Emperor Vespasian around A.D. 70-72. It was opened in A.D. 80 by Vespasian's son Titus and was officially known as the Flavian Amphitheater.

Churches

The Vatican

The Vatican is the head of the church and is located in Vatican City. Vatican City is in Rome but is actually its own country. The Vatican is home of the pope and has many beautiful paintings and statues. One the most famous works of art is the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel painted by Michelangelo.
